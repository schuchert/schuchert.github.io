tension nodes). Disengaging the lock allows the system to transition to liquid alignment.

### Zero-Drop Foundation

The structural "Null State" achieved when the Broomstick Lock is fully released. The skeleton hangs naturally from the
fascial net without active muscular bracing. It requires trusting the internal architecture to hold the system upright
rather than micromanaging gravity via the explicit cognitive interface.

### Visceral Interference Patterns

Deliberately induced kinetic disruptions designed to reset thixotropic fluids and release fascial tension. Crucially,
this is *not* high-frequency, high-BPM "shaking." It is a precise structural perturbation applied exactly at the
turnaround points of movement or breath to prevent the fascia from locking. It is the physical application of a system
dither.

groundedness. The natural, unavoidable byproduct of this groundedness—and the lack of internal rushing—is an outward
tone of calm, unshakeable authority.

### The Inner Critic as Status Broadcast

The "Inner Critic" is not a personification, an alter-ego, or a psychological demon to be conquered. It is simply a
systemic status broadcast—a log listener—reporting on the internal noise floor. When system noise is high (due to
structural tension or autonomic dysregulation), the broadcast is loud. As the somatic noise floor drops, the broadcast
naturally quiets because there are no errors to report. You do not argue with the broadcast; you lower the noise floor.

### P2P Handshake

Peer-to-peer relational connection. Clean system-to-system communication (with a partner, an AI, or an environment) is
only possible when internal noise is low. If the local node is highly stable, it can engage in complex co-processing
without coercive control or friction. If the node is unstable, the handshake fails and drops packets.

### The Fractal Expanse

The principle that internal states resonate outward. The micro dictates the macro. Internal conductance creates external
conductance; internal chaos mirrors external chaos. By stabilizing the local node (the self) down to the 0.19 threshold,
the surrounding relational field—the broader Egregore—is inherently and unavoidably altered.
